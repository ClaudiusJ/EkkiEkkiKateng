EkkiEkkiKateng is a program that creates build-files (currently only for
CodeBlocks) out of project descriptions written in EScript.

[https://github.com/ClaudiusJ/EkkiEkkiKateng]
[https://github.com/EScript]
[http://www.codeblocks.org]
